Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior coding experience?

No. We provide crash courses and mentorship for beginners. The competitions are designed to be accessible for everyone, regardless of your technical background.

What if I don't have a partner?

We will pair you with another student before the competition begins. Just indicate that you need a partner when you register through the WhatsApp group.

Will participation certificates be given?

Yes, every participant will receive a digital certificate. Top performers will receive special recognition certificates in addition to their prizes.
